# Introduction

The **Integrated Clean Cooking Planning Tool (ICCPT)** — is a techno-economic, environmental, and social simulation framework designed to support **national-level planning for clean-cooking transitions**.

It provides a unified structure to evaluate the evolution of access to modern energy for cooking and electrification, considering both **technical feasibility** and **socio-economic constraints**.

---

# 🌍 Introduction

## 💡 What is ICCPT

The **Integrated Clean Cooking Planning Tool (ICCPT)** is an open-source, techno-economic and social simulation tool that supports **national planning for the transition to clean cooking**.

It combines data on **energy demand, income, technology adoption, costs, emissions, and social impacts** to simulate how households and regions can progressively shift from traditional to modern cooking fuels and technologies.

ICCPT provides a **transparent, modular, and replicable framework** that helps countries assess their current situation, explore transition pathways, and evaluate the implications of different policy or investment decisions for achieving **universal access to clean cooking**.

---

## 🎯 Objective of the Tool

The purpose of ICCPT is to enable **evidence-based decision-making** for clean-cooking transitions.  
It does so by:

- Integrating **technical, economic, and social dimensions** of clean-cooking transitions into a single analytical framework.  
- Quantifying **adoption dynamics** of fuels and appliances across rural and urban contexts.  
- Estimating **costs, emissions, and social impacts**, including CAPEX, OPEX, health benefits, time savings, gender, emissions, deforestation.  
- Generating **actionable outputs** that can be visualized, compared, and exported in standard formats (TSV, Excel, GIS-compatible).  
- Supporting **national planning** with consistent, customizable, country-level data and parameters.

In short, ICCPT offers governments, researchers, and international partners a **decision-support environment** for designing realistic, cost-effective, and socially equitable clean-cooking strategies.

---

## 👥 Target Audience

ICCPT is designed for a wide range of users involved in **clean-cooking policy, analysis, and implementation**:

| Profile | How they use ICCPT |
|----------|--------------------|
| **Government ministries and agencies** | Develop or test national clean-cooking strategies, define targets, and prioritize investment. |
| **Energy planners and analysts** | Simulate adoption patterns, compare technology mixes, and quantify costs and emissions. |
| **Researchers and universities** | Explore behavioural, socio-economic, and spatial dynamics of fuel adoption within a replicable framework. |
| **International organizations and donors** | Evaluate progress toward SDG 7 and design interventions that accelerate clean-cooking adoption. |

---

## 🌱 Why ICCPT Matters

Access to clean cooking remains a **mayor global challenge**, with direct implications for health, gender equality, and environmental sustainability.  
ICCPT contributes to addressing this challenge by providing a **quantitative, country-specific tool** that:

- Offers a **systematic assessment** of costs and benefits for different transition scenarios.  
- Bridges **research and implementation**, ensuring that technical data informs national decisions.  
- Encourages **transparency and comparability** across countries and policy contexts.
